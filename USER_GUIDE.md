# Blind Assistive Head Tech – User Guide

## Introduction

The Blind Assistive Head Tech is a wearable headband designed to help visually impaired individuals navigate safely and independently. The device uses ultrasonic sensors to detect obstacles and provides feedback through vibration and voice guidance.

This guide explains how to properly wear, operate, and use the device.

---

## 1. Wearing the Headband

### Step 1: Place the Headband on Your Head

- Wear the device like a regular headband.
- Make sure the sensors are facing forward.
- Position the headband comfortably above your ears.

### Step 2: Adjust the Fit

- At the back of the headband, there is an adjustable strap.
- Adjust the tightness based on your comfort.
- Make sure the device is secure but not too tight.

---

## 2. Turning the Device On and Off

### Power Switch Location

- The power switch is located on the right side of the headband near the ear.
- The switch is positioned on the top side of the device.

### To Turn ON the Device

- Push the switch backward.
- The device will power on.
- Once the device is turned on, it will speak: **"DEVICE READY"**
- This voice confirmation lets the user know that the device is successfully powered on and ready to use.
- After hearing "DEVICE READY", you may proceed to the next step to learn about the button functions and controls.

### To Turn OFF the Device

- Push the switch forward.
- The device will shut down.

---

## 3. Button Layout and Functions

The left side of the headband has three buttons:

- **Front Button**
- **Middle Button**
- **Back Button**

---

## 4. Front Button – "Where Am I?" Function

- Press the front button to activate the location feature.
- The device will provide location information using voice guidance.

---

## 5. Middle Button Functions

### Short Press Functions

**Vibration Increase**
- Short press the middle button to increase vibration intensity.
- This works only when:
  - Vibration mode is ON
  - Voice/Speak function is OFF

**Volume Increase**
- Short press the middle button to increase speaker volume.
- This works only when:
  - Voice/Speak function is ON
  - Vibration mode is OFF

### Long Press Function

- Press and hold the middle button for at least 3 seconds.
- This will:
  - Activate vibration mode, or
  - Deactivate vibration mode

---

## 6. Back Button Functions

### Short Press Functions

**Vibration Decrease**
- Short press the back button to decrease vibration intensity.
- This works only when:
  - Vibration mode is ON
  - Voice/Speak function is OFF

**Volume Decrease**
- Short press the back button to decrease speaker volume.
- This works only when:
  - Voice/Speak function is ON
  - Vibration mode is OFF

### Long Press Function

- Press and hold the back button for at least 3 seconds.
- This will:
  - Activate the voice/speak function, or
  - Deactivate the voice/speak function

---

## 7. Saved Settings Memory

The device automatically saves the last active settings even after turning the device off.

### Example 1

If the device is turned off while BOTH vibration and speak functions are ON:
- When turned on again:
- BOTH functions will remain ON.

### Example 2

If the device is turned off while ONLY vibration mode is ON:
- When turned on again:
- ONLY vibration mode will remain active.

### Example 3

If the device is turned off while ONLY speak mode is ON:
- When turned on again:
- ONLY speak mode will remain active.

---

## 8. Vibration Detection System

When vibration mode is activated, the sensors continuously scan:

- Front
- Left
- Right
- Front-down (ground-level)

### Obstacle Detection

The device can detect:

- Walls
- People
- Objects
- Ground hazards

### Vibration Intensity Meaning

- **Soft vibration** = Obstacle is far away
- **Strong vibration** = Obstacle is very near

### Detection Range

Vibration alerts begin at approximately 200 centimeters from the user.

---

## 9. Voice/Speak Function

When the speak function is activated:

- The device uses the front sensor for distance reading.
- The device announces the detected distance in centimeters.

### Example

If the device says: **"150"**
- It means the obstacle is 150 centimeters away.

### Danger Alert

When the obstacle is 60 centimeters or closer, the device will announce:
- **"DANGER"**
- This means the obstacle is very near and immediate caution is required.

---

## 10. Using Vibration and Speak Together

Users can activate:

- Vibration only
- Speak only
- Both vibration and speak together

Choose the mode that is most comfortable and suitable for your environment.

---

## 11. Safety Reminders

- Always wear the headband properly before use.
- Ensure the sensors are not blocked.
- Keep the device clean and dry.
- Do not expose the device to water.
- Recharge or replace the power source as needed.
- Use the device together with proper mobility awareness.

---

## 12. Quick Start Summary

1. Wear the headband.
2. Adjust the fit comfortably.
3. Push the right-side switch backward to turn ON.
4. Wait for the voice message: **"DEVICE READY"**
5. Choose vibration mode, speak mode, or both.
6. Use the device for safe navigation.
7. Push the switch forward to turn OFF.
